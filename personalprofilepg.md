# Personal Profile Page — Brainstorm Questions

This file contains brainstormed questions to help build out a personal profile page. Use these as prompts for content generation, self-reflection, or interview-style answers.

---

## 🧑 Identity & Introduction

- Who are you? (Name, nickname, handle — however you go by)
- What's your tagline or one-liner? (Keep it sharp)
- What's your current role / title / thing you do?
- Where are you based? (City, timezone, remote?)
- What's your vibe in 5 words or less?
- What emoji represents you right now?

## 💼 Professional / What You Do

- What do you actually do? (Not your job title — what you _do_)
- What's your core skill / superpower?
- What kind of problems do you solve?
- Who do you work with / who's your audience?
- What's a project you're proud of?
- What tools / stack / medium do you work in?
- What's something you're currently learning or leveling up in?
- What's a career highlight so far?

## 🎨 Personality & Flavor

- What's something people get wrong about you?
- What's a hot take you stand by?
- What's a guilty pleasure (media, food, habit)?
- What's your go-to conversation starter?
- What's a quote, mantra, or line you live by?
- If your life had a soundtrack, what's the opening track?
- What's your default coffee / tea / drink order?
- What's something that makes you unreasonably happy?
- What's the weirdest thing about you?

## 🌐 Social & Presence

- Where can people find you? (Links: GitHub, Twitter, LinkedIn, blog, etc.)
- What's your preferred platform for chat / work / posting?
- Are you open to DMs / collaborations / freelance?
- What's your response time like? (Realistic, not aspirational)

## 🎯 Goals & Why

- What are you building toward right now?
- What's a goal for the next 6 months? 1 year?
- What kind of people / projects do you want to attract?
- What's the "why" behind what you do?
- What does success look like to you?

## 💡 Fun / Icebreakers

- If you could have dinner with anyone (dead or alive), who?
- What's a hill you'll die on? (Tech, food, opinion, doesn't matter)
- What's your most controversial opinion?
- Cats or dogs? (Both is not an answer)
- What's a skill you want to learn just for fun?
- What's the best piece of advice you've ever gotten?
- What's something you've changed your mind about recently?

## 🖼 Content & Media Ideas

- Headshot / avatar style preference?
- Do you want a background banner / hero image? What would it be?
- Any logos, brands, or badges you want to feature?
- Links to your best work / portfolio items?
- Embedded music / video / interactive elements?

---

## 📦 Suggested Sections (To Organize Answers)

| Section           | Purpose                                      |
|-------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| **Headline**      | Name + tagline + emoji                       |
| **About**         | Who you are + what you do + your vibe        |
| **Work**          | Skills, projects, highlights, tools          |
| **Personality**   | Hot takes, favorites, quirks                 |
| **Links**         | Social / contact / portfolio                 |
| **Goals**         | What you're building toward                  |
| **Fun**           | Icebreakers / conversation starters          |

---

## 🔄 How to Use

1. Pick the questions that resonate with you.
2. Answer them in your own voice — raw, no editing.
3. Use those answers to generate the actual profile page copy.
4. Update this file with your answers or create a separate `answers.md`.
