# 🍌 Banana

A banana is an elongated, edible fruit produced by several kinds of large herbaceous flowering plants in the genus *Musa*.

## Fun Facts
- Bananas are berries botanically (strawberries are not)
- They grow on plants that are technically giant herbs, not trees
- The most common type is the Cavendish
- Bananas contain potassium, vitamin B6, vitamin C, and manganese
- A cluster of bananas is called a "hand" with individual ones called "fingers"
