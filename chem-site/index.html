🧪 How Bonding Works

🤝

Covalent Bonding

Two non-metals sharing electrons equally. It's about balance — what's mine is yours. Low electronegativity difference? You've got a stable relationship.

"Sharing is caring 💕"
⚡

Ionic Bonding

A metal gives an electron to a non-metal. One loses, one gains. Complete opposites attract, and the electrostatic attraction is undeniable. High electronegativity difference = fireworks.

"Opposites attract 🔥"
🌊

Metallic Bonding

A sea of delocalized electrons shared between metal cations. It's complicated, it's fluid, and nobody really knows who's with who — but it works.

"It's an open relationship 🤷"
🧊

Hydrogen Bonding

A special type of intermolecular force. Not quite a full bond, but strong enough to hold water molecules together — and make DNA possible. The situationship of chemistry.

"It's complicated 💧"
